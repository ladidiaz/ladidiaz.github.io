Palette and Plate Gallery was founded in 1974 by two young best friends, Jocena Grace and Will Green. Jocena was an up and coming artist in the LA area who had a minimalist approach in her art and with that approach it was hard to get her work in galleries. Will, her best friend was a chef and always wanted to be the head chef at his own restaurant and so he brought the idea of him and Jocena partnering to open a restaurant where he could live his dream as head chef and she could display her art in the restaurant. From that day on they decided that they would only go where they are valued and feel important and they also wanted to create that same environment for their customers. That mindset is how they brought fine dining and art together at
Palette and Plate Gallery.
